Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Templates & Design

How do I change my CV template? +
To change your CV template, go to the "Design" tab in the editor, browse through available templates, and click "Apply" on the one you like. Your content will automatically adjust to the new template.
Can I customize colors and fonts? +
Yes! In the "Customize" section, you can change colors, fonts, spacing, and other design elements. We offer 20+ font families and unlimited color options.
How do I add my photo to the CV? +
Click on the photo placeholder in your template, then upload your photo from your device. We recommend using a professional headshot with good lighting.

Technical Issues

My work is not saving. What should I do? +
First, check your internet connection. If the issue persists, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. All work is auto-saved every 30 seconds.
The editor is loading slowly. How can I fix this? +
Try clearing your browser cache, disable browser extensions, or use incognito mode. For best performance, we recommend Chrome or Firefox.
How do I export my CV as PDF? +
Click the "Download" button in the top right, select "PDF", choose your preferences (size, margins), and click "Export". The download will start automatically.

Account & Billing

How do I cancel my subscription? +
Go to Account Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription. You can cancel anytime, and you'll keep access until the end of your billing period.
Can I use CV Builder on multiple devices? +
Yes! Your account syncs across all devices. Simply log in from any computer, tablet, or smartphone to access your CVs.
How do I reset my password? +
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions in the email we send you.
