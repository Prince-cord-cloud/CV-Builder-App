Frequently Asked Questions

Note: You now need to create an account to use our CV builder program. This allows us to save your work and offer more personalized features.

Is it free to use?

Yes! Our CV builder is completely free for everyone. Creating an account is also free and gives you access to all features.

Why do I need an account now?

Accounts allow us to save your CV templates, progress, and settings across devices. This ensures you never lose your work and can access it anytime.

Can I download multiple templates?

Yes, with an account you can choose different templates and download each CV in PDF format. Your previous templates are saved in your account.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and never share your personal information. Your CV data is only accessible through your secure account.

How do I create an account?

Simply click the "Sign Up" button at the top right. You can register with your email, Google account, or LinkedIn profile in just a few seconds.

Can I delete my account?

Yes, you can delete your account anytime from the settings page. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.
